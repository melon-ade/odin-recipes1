Brownies

Description

Delicious homemade brownies that taste boxed!

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Powdered sugar
  • Chocolate chips
  • Sea salt
  • Eggs
  • Canola Oil
  • Water
  • Vanilla

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325F
  2. Combine dry ingredients
  3. Combine wet ingredients
  4. Combine all ingredients
  5. Grease a pan then place it into oven
