Snickers Dates

Description

Dates filled with peanut butter and covered with chocolate!

Ingredients

  • Pitted Dates
  • Peanut Butter
  • Chocolate
  • Chopped Peanuts
  • Coconut Oil
  • Flaky Salt

Steps

  1. Add peanut butter into pitted dates
  2. Melt chocolate with coconut oil
  3. While it hardens, add chopped peanuts and flaky salt
  4. Enjoy!
